# Easy Eats – IA Presentation Pitch Notes

---

# 1. Introduction

Good morning everyone.

We are Team Easy Eats, and our project focuses on solving one common student problem on campus:

**Long queues and wasted time while buying food.**

Students often lose valuable break time waiting at crowded food counters.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Current campus food systems face:

- Long waiting lines
- Manual order confusion
- Slow service
- Limited payment options
- No live tracking
- Poor user experience

---

# 3. Our Solution

We built **Easy Eats**, a smart campus food ordering platform.

Students can:

- Login securely
- Browse restaurants
- Add food to cart
- Pay online
- Track orders live
- Pick up food quickly

---

# 4. Key Features

## Student Features

- Secure login
- Fast ordering
- Razorpay payment
- Live tracking
- Responsive design

## Admin Features

- Manage incoming orders
- Update preparation status
- Complete orders dashboard

## Smart Features

- Chatbot support
- Mobile optimized UI

---

# 5. Technology Stack

# 6. Customer Validation

We interviewed students and found:

- Most students hate waiting in queues
- UPI payment preferred
- Strong interest in pre-order system

We also conducted surveys.
