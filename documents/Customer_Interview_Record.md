# Customer Interview Record  
## Easy Eats – Campus Food Ordering Platform

---

# 1. Purpose of Interviews

The purpose of conducting customer interviews was to understand real problems faced by students while purchasing food on campus and to validate whether a digital ordering platform like Easy Eats would solve those issues.

A total of **3 sample customer interviews** were conducted with college students.

---

# Interview 1

## Customer Details

- Name: Rahul Sharma  
- Age: 20  
- Course: CSE  
- Year: 2nd Year

## Questions & Responses

### Q1. How often do you buy food on campus?

Almost every day during lunch break.

### Q2. What problems do you face?

Long queues and waiting time. Sometimes break ends before I get food.

### Q3. Preferred payment method?

UPI payment.

### Q4. Would you use a pre-order app?

Yes, definitely.

### Q5. Most wanted feature?

Quick pickup without standing in line.

## Key Insight

Students value time savings more than anything else.

---

# Interview 2

---

# Summary of Findings

Based on all three interviews:

## Common Problems Identified

- Long waiting lines
- Counter crowding
- Delayed service
- No order tracking
- Menu confusion
- Need for digital payment

## Common Requested Features

- Pre-order food
- Quick pickup
- Live tracking
- UPI payments
- Notifications

---

# Validation Result

All interviewed users showed positive interest in using Easy Eats if implemented on campus.

This validates the product-market fit for a smart campus food ordering solution.
